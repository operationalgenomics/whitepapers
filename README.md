## ðŸ§  What is Operational Genomics?

Operational Genomics is the discipline that treats operational knowledge as an 
**evolutive and intentional organism**, encoded through:

- Praxeological Codons  
- Operational Genes  
- Digital Genomes  
- Cognitive Cores  
- Universal Neutral Language (UNL)

Its goal is to overcome industrial fragmentation and enable coherent, explainable, 
real-time intelligent action across complex environments.
